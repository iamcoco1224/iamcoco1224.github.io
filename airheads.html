Penny will come to life and invite you to play with her when you see her through your phone. Using AR technology, you can interact with the Airhead Gummy you created and share it with your friends! To further push the "PLAY" element, we also created the Bus Stop Interactive Ads which has facial recognition. It will detect your facial expression and turn you into an ideantical Airheads Gummy!
