Designer Dove #searchbeauty

Dove
#searchbeauty

The task Create a campaign as an extension of the current campaign.

The deliverables Pre-roll ad scripts, video productions, print ads and media plan.

The client Dove. For over a decade, Dove has been working to make beauty a source of confidence, not anxiety, and together our journey continues. Beauty is not defined by shape, size or color – it’s feeling like the best version of yourself. Authentic. Unique. Real. 

The Big idea Search beauty in you.

The campaign In today’s world, we are consistently consuming information that may or may not fall under our consciousness. Under the circumstances, there is a fat chance that we have been projected certain values and standards imperceptibly. This campaign aims to point out the insecurities caused by those values, and to break them. These three pre-roll ads present the insecurities and the frequency we reach out on the internet for “beauty” and tell the story about how Dove believes the beauty is in you, not anywhere else. As for the print ad and display ad, it delivers the same message in a more direct way that “beauty is in you.”
