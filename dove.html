Dove
#searchbeauty

The Task  Create a campaign as an extension of the current campaign.

The Deliverables  Pre-roll ad scripts, video productions, print ads and media plan.

The Client  Dove. For over a decade, Dove has been working to make beauty a source of confidence, not anxiety, and together our journey continues. Beauty is not defined by shape, size or color – it’s feeling like the best version of yourself. Authentic. Unique. Real. 

The Big idea  Look for beauty within.

The Campaign In today’s world, we are constantly consuming information, almost unconsciously. Under the circumstances, there is a fat chance that certain values and standards have been projected onto us imperceptibly. This campaign aims to point out the insecurities caused by those values, and to break them. These three pre-roll ads present our insecurities, and the frequency with which we look to the internet to discover “beauty,” to tell how Dove believes the beauty is in you — not anywhere else. As for the print ads and display ads, they strengthen the message that “beauty is in you.”
