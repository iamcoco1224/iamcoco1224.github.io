Eat in Taiwan
Web Design

This is a 7-day mini project to build a website that tells about a part of me. There’s an old saying goes “people who are having their meals should be treated like kings”. This phrase shows how Taiwanese value food and their meal time. The accessibility of all kinds of cuisine is way beyond imagination in Taiwan. Therefore, I have always believed that the best way to get to know Taiwan is through its food. This website will walk you through every corner of Taiwan with good food from day to night. I intend to complete the content of this website. Please email me if you are interested in certain topics and/or would like me to write anything in.
