Multi-function Cleaner

Every choice we make in our daily life affects our environment and the detergent we choose to use is no different. Detergent is always related to chemicals and environmental irresponsibility. The truth is, cleaning products can clean efficiently without any chemical ingredient. Oopz citrus oil extract is natural and green. It can be discharged into a field or river, which also means that this product will bring harm neither to your beautiful skin nor your loved ones. Let’s keep our family away from irresponsible cleaning products and keep the land clean as she deserves.
Sustainability

Oopz's goal is to produce products that have no harm to our surroundings. The sustainability of the environment is our major responsibility.

Eco-Friendly

Oopz Citrus Oil Extract derived natural oil from citrus peel which provides efficient cleaning in removing grease, tape residue and glue.

All-Purpose

Oopz Citrus Oil Extract is a all-purpose cleaner. It can be used in cleaning dishes, kitchen, bathroom and laundry.
