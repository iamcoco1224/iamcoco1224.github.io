Entrepreneur Oopz! Citrus Oil

Oopz! Citrus Oil
Multi-function Cleaner

Every choice we make in our daily lives affects our environment and the detergent we choose to use is no different. Detergent is always related to chemicals and environmental irresponsibility. The truth is, cleaning products can clean efficiently without any chemical ingredients. Oopz! Citrus Oil Extract is natural and green. It can be discharged into a field or river, which also means that this product will bring harm neither to your beautiful skin nor your loved ones. Let’s keep our families away from irresponsible cleaning products and keep the land clean as she deserves.
Sustainability

Our goal is to produce products that do no harm to our surroundings. Environmental sustainability is our major responsibility.

Eco-Friendly

Oopz Citrus Oil Extract is derived from natural oils from citrus peels which provide efficient cleaning in removing grease, tape residue and glue.

All-Purpose

Oopz Citrus Oil Extract is an all-purpose cleaner. It can be used to cleaning dishes, kitchens, bathrooms and clothes.
