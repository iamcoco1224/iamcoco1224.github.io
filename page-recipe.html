    Radish Cake

     star star star star star_half (201)
    4 ingredients | 8 servings | 60 minutes
    chinese chinese chinese chinese
    recipe by Coco Chan

    Ingredients

    • 1. Radish 1lb
    • 2. Green onion
    • 2. Rice flour 500g
    • 3. Red chilli (optional)

    Where to start

    • 1. Cut the radish into small pieces.
    • 2. Boil a liter of water and put the radigh in.
    • 2. Cook until the radish turn tender.
    • 3. Add in rice flour and gentally stir it.
    • 4. Steam the mix for about 60 mins.

    camera_altsee photos

    Tips

  • Pour the rice flour once a little into the radish water, and gentally stir it until its perfectly mixed. Use chopstick to check if the mix is cooked.

    Story behind it

    This radish cake is a muct-have in Chinese New Year. I love its simple taste. It represents that ones can reach a higher status in the new year. I like it so much since it's also my mom's favorite dish and she will make us this within the whole New Year's holidays.
