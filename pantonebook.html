Designer Pantone Color System Guide (Tea Egg)

Pantone Color System Guide
How to make Tea Eggs

Project Objective  This project is to learn how the Pantone system works and design a mini booklet to introduce it. The Pantone color system is not only a color guide, but also an important index for the industry and market. Therefore, I came up with three initial ideas for the book:

Inspiration  Colors control our emotions and appetites as well. I always find the natural colors of food are magical in that they can provoke people’s desire to eat them. Being not only a foodie with a strong passion for cuisine, but also an adventurous cook has pushed me to choose this theme to introduce the Pantone color system.

This book will walk the reader through how the Pantone Color System works, while it also teaches how to make Taiwanese tea eggs.
