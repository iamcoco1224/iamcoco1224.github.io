Designer Pantone Color System Guide (Tea Egg)

Pantone Color System Guide
-How to make Tea Eggs

The objective of this project is to learn how the Pantone system works and design a mini booklet to introduce it. The Pantone color system is not only a color guide, but also an important index for the industry and market. Therefore, I came up with three initial ideas for the book:

1.Red in the city: I spent a summer in Boston and noticed that the color red has been elegantly presented in every corner of the city. Pictures I took in Boston remind me of the atmosphere and power Pantone color images have delivered throughout the years. Hence, I want to introduce the Pantone color system with the theme of “The Red in Boston.”

2.Fashion guide: Pantone is also the major color guide in the fashion field. Being a fashion designer as well, I think it will be cohesive and relevant to talk about the Pantone color system while designing a fashion guide for Spring 2019.

3.A recipe: Colors control our emotions and appetites as well. I always find the natural colors of food are magical in that they can provoke people’s desire to eat them. Being not only a foodie with a strong passion for cuisine, but also an adventurous cook has pushed me to choose this theme to introduce the Pantone color system.

This book will walk the reader through how the Pantone Color System works, while it also teaches how to make Taiwanese tea eggs.
