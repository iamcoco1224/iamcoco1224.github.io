Red Crush
Chinese Calligraphy Workshop

We are ringing in the year of pig on February 5th, 2019 and there will be a quarter of the world celebrating the Lanar New Year. RED CRUSH will provide lessons and workshops for patrons to learn the history of Lunar New Year and its customs. Most importantly, they can get their hands on how to create their own red couplets. The event will focus on workshopping the Chinese red couplets and calligraphy. If you are craftsmen and artist, innovative and creative, adventurous and ambitious, this is where you belong! This event has absolutely no limit to people with different age, ethnicity and nationality; people can come and simply enjoy arts and history stories.
